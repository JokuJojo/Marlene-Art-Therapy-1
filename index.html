Welcome to Marlene Art Therapy, a place where creativity meets healing.

Whether you're seeking personal growth, emotional healing, or simply a safe space to express yourself, we're here to guide you on your journey. Art therapy offers a unique and powerful way to explore your inner world, reduce stress, and enhance well-being through the transformative power of artistic expression. Join us in discovering the profound connection between art and healing, and take the first step toward a more balanced and fulfilled life.

Who are we?

We are a compassionate team of licensed art therapists dedicated to helping individuals tap into their creativity for emotional and mental well-being. With a deep understanding of the healing potential of art, we provide a safe, supportive space for self-expression and personal growth. Our mission is to empower you to explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences through the transformative process of creating art.

Our Goal

Our goal is to help you connect with your inner self, overcome emotional challenges, and cultivate a sense of peace and resilience. Through guided art therapy sessions, we aim to support you in expressing what words may not capture, promoting healing, personal growth, and a deeper understanding of yourself. We believe in the therapeutic power of creativity and strive to help you unlock it in a way that fosters both emotional healing and lasting change.

About Marlene

Marlene is a 58-year old woman with a diverse background in painting, sculpture, felting and more. After experiencing personal trauma, she turned to art for healing, which inspired her to study art therapy. Seeking a peaceful and focused environment, she built a studio away from the city, offering a serene space for creative expression. With her empathetic and non-judgmental approach, Marlene is dedicated to helping others use art to heal,grow, and discover themselves, providing a compassionate and supportive space for emotional well-being.
